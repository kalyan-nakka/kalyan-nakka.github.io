Bio

I am a Ph.D. student in the Department of Computer Science & Engineering, Texas A&M University at College Station (TAMU). I am advised by Dr. Nitesh Saxena at SPIES research lab. My current research interest lies in the intersection of Deep Learning (DL), Large Language Models (LLMs) and Adversarial Machine Learning domains, with a slight use of Computational Geometry.

I received my M.S. degree (Thesis) from Texas A&M University at Kingsville (TAMUK) in 2023. At TAMUK, I had the oppurtunity to work with Dr. Habib M. Ammari at WiSeMAN Research Lab, on the problem of reliable and fault-tolerant coverage in Wireless Sensor Networks, and with Dr. Taesic Kim at CPPES Lab, on the problems pertaining to Cybersecurity of Smart Grid. Prior to this, I received my B.Tech. degree from Indian Institute of Technology (IIT) at Dhanbad in 2016. Also, I'm a Mid-Senior level professional with 5 years of Software Engineering experience from 2016 to 2021.
