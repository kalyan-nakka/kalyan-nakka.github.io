Bio

I am an incoming Ph.D. student at the Department of Computer Science & Engineering, Texas A&M University at College Station (TAMU). I will be advised by Dr. Nitesh Saxena at SPIES research lab. My current research interest lies in the intersection of Cybersecurity, Machine Learning, Artificial Intelligence and Computational Geometry domains.

I received my M.S. degree (Thesis) from Texas A&M University at Kingsville (TAMUK) in 2023. I was advised by Dr. Habib M. Ammari at WiSeMAN Research Lab, where I worked in the development of energy-efficient and fault-tolerant Wireless Sensor Networks leveraging Computational Geometry-based approaches. Prior to this, I received my B.Tech. degree from Indian Institute of Technology (IIT) at Dhanbad in 2016.
